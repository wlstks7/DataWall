Methods (All Files)

Unauthorized immigration and legal permanent residents from Mexico by the nominal count. (File)

Using the unauthorized immigrant populations PDFs, we recorded the country birth of the unauthorized immigrant population for that year from Mexico. Take that number and divide it by the state of residence of the unauthorized immigtant population for all states. The decimal result is the percentage of unauthorized illegal immigrants from Mexico versus all immigration.

Percentage of unauthorized immigration and legal permanent residents from Mexico into Arizona versus the other states. (File)

Using the legal permanent residence estimates PDFs, we recorded the country birth of the LPR population for that year from Mexico. Take that number and divide it by the state of residence of the legal permanent resident population for all states. The decimal result is the percentage of legal permanent residents from Mexico versus all immigration.
